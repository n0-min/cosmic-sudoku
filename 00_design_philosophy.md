# COSMIC LOGIC — Design Philosophy

**Movement Name**: "Stellar Calculus"

## I. The Premise
Space is not emptiness — it is a system of invisible forces made visible through their effects. Light bends, time dilates, matter organizes itself into geometric perfection. Stellar Calculus borrows this premise: structure and beauty are not opposites but the same truth seen from different distances. Every grid, every number, every interaction is a gravitational event — precise, consequential, beautiful.

## II. Space, Form & Void
The canvas lives in deep darkness, not as absence but as infinite potential. Elements float in carefully governed negative space, as if placed by orbital mechanics rather than human hands. The sudoku grid is not a table — it is a star map, each cell a celestial coordinate. Three-cell block boundaries pulse with soft luminescence, like nebula edges catching starlight. All spacing follows the golden ratio: painstakingly calibrated to feel both vast and intimate.

## III. Color as Cosmic Phenomenon
The palette evolves through the progression arc: Earth (muted terrestrial blues, 0A0E1A to 1A2744) → Moon (silver-grey luminescence, 8A9BB8) → Mars (oxidized copper and rust undertones, C0553A) → deep space (electric violet, neon cyan #00F5FF, gold #FFD700). These are not decorative colors — they are the visual language of physics: the spectral signature of each level's star system. Tension states trigger chromatic shifts from deep indigo to warning crimson, a visceral chromatic alarm that feels inevitable.

## IV. Rhythm & Material
Typography is dual-natured: Tektur carries the monumental weight of section titles — angular, unapologetic, forged. Jura whispers the scientific precision of UI labels — cool, rational, unhurried. JetBrains Mono renders numbers as data-points in a living system. Each skin is a distinct material reality: Nebula is diffuse and gaseous (radial gradients, soft glow); Black Hole is dense and warped (distortion grids, pure black absorption); Supernova is violent light (sharp rays, explosive aureoles); Aurora is liquid and iridescent (shifting horizontal bands); Quantum is crystalline (grid interference patterns, green phosphor); Void is ceremonial silence (hairline grids on absolute darkness).

## V. Craftsmanship Imperative
This design demands the painstaking care of a master watchmaker. Every pixel carries intention. Glow radiuses are computed, not estimated. Color stops in gradients are placed with the precision of scientific measurement. The result must feel as though it emerged from a discipline where imprecision is catastrophic — because in space, it is. No element is placed casually. No spacing is approximate. Every interaction state, every transition, every micro-animation is the product of deep expertise and countless hours of refinement. This is design that could hang in a museum as a diagram of something that actually matters.
